# Physical Enhancement & Spirituality by Pathway

Each sequence entry lists the **Spirituality** (max spirituality pool) and the **Physical Enhancement** stats granted passively at that sequence level.

**Enhancement stat meanings:**
- **Strength** — +3 attack damage per level
- **Speed** — +0.02 movement speed per level
- **Health** — +4 max HP per level
- **Regeneration** — Regeneration effect at that level
- **Resistance** — ~5% damage reduction per level (hard-capped at 100%)
- **Fire Resistance** — Fire immunity levels (level 1 = full immunity)
- **Night Vision** — Permanent night vision
- **Underwater Breathing** — Permanent water breathing
- **Dolphins Grace** — Swim speed bonus
- **Conduit** — Conduit power effect
- **Luck** — Luck effect
