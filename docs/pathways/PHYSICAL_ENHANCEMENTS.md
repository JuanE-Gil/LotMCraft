# Physical Enhancements & Spirituality by Pathway

Each pathway grants passive physical bonuses at every sequence. Stats shown are the **total modifiers** applied at that sequence level.

## Stat Reference

| Stat | Per Level Value |
|------|----------------|
| Strength | +3.0 attack damage per level |
| Speed | +0.02 movement speed per level |
| Health | +4.0 max HP per level |
| Resistance | 5% damage reduction per level (stacks additively, capped at 100%) |
| Regeneration | Potion amplifier = level − 1 (Regen I at level 1, Regen II at level 2, etc.) |
| Luck | Luck potion effect level |
| Night Vision | Permanent Night Vision effect |
| Fire Resistance | Permanent Fire Resistance effect |
| Underwater Breathing | Infinite air supply |
| Dolphins Grace | Permanent Dolphins Grace effect |
| Conduit | Permanent Conduit Power effect |

Stat bonuses shown as **level numbers** (e.g. Str 3 = +9.0 attack damage, HP 16 = +64 max HP).

## Notes

### Characteristic Stacks (Health bonus)
Each characteristic stack digested at a given sequence adds bonus HP levels on top of the base:

| Seq digested | Extra HP levels per stack |
|-------------|--------------------------|
| 8 | +1 |
| 7 | +2 |
| 6 | +3 |
| 5 | +3 |
| 4 | +5 |
| 3 | +5 |
| 2 | +7 |
| 1 | +7 |

### Saturation
All Beyonders at Sequence 4 and below have their hunger and saturation permanently filled.

### Pathways not yet implemented
The following pathways do not have physical enhancement implementations yet: White Tower, Hanged Man, Death, Twilight Giant, Hermit, Paragon, Moon, Chained, Black Emperor, Justiciar.
