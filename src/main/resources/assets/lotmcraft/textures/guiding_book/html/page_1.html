I

To become a Beyonder is to walk a Path of transformation — of body, mind, and soul — through the sacred art of potion-craft.

The Rites of Ascension
  • Step I Choose a single Pathway. All potions must belong to the same lineage — mixing paths leads to ruin.
  • Step II Begin at Sequence 9. Brew your potion in a Brewing Cauldron with the required ingredients and recipe.
  • Step III The main ingredient may be swapped for the matching Beyonder Characteristic of that sequence.
  • Step IV After drinking, fully digest the potion before the next may be safely taken.
  • Step V Once digested, proceed to Sequence 8, then 7 — climbing onward, one step at a time.
