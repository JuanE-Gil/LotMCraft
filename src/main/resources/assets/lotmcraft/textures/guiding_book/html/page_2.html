As a Beyonder grows in power, so too do the gifts of their Pathway manifest — abilities both subtle and terrible, channelled through the will and spirit of their wielder.

Preparing the Ability Wheel
  • Step I Press J to open the Introspect Menu and navigate to the Abilities tab.
  • Step II Drag your abilities from the list into the slots of the Ability Wheel to assign them.
  • Step III In-game, hold Alt to toggle the wheel open, or hold Y to show it only while held.
  • Step IV Hover over the desired ability on the wheel to select it, then press M to activate it.

Spirituality & Cooldowns

Each ability draws upon your inner reserves of Spirituality — the vital essence of every Beyonder. Without sufficient Spirituality, an ability cannot be invoked, no matter the urgency of the moment.

Furthermore, many abilities impose a cooldown after use, during which they may not be called upon again. Manage both wisely in the heat of confrontation.

Keybind Reference
  • J Open the Introspect Menu & Abilities tab
  • Alt Toggle the Ability Wheel open / closed
  • Y Show the Ability Wheel while held
  • M Activate the currently selected ability
